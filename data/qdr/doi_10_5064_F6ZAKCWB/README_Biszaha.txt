**Title**
Data For: Exploring Clinician Expectations and Preferences of Library Subject Guides

**Suggested Citation**
Please cite any data used from this data project as follows: 

Biszaha, Anna. 2025. "Data For: Exploring Clinician Expectations and Preferences of Library Subject Guides". Qualitative Data Repository. https://doi.org/10.5064/F6ZAKCWB. QDR Main Collection. 

**Description**
11 semi-structured interviews were conducted with clinicians to better understand their expectations and preferences of library subject guides.  This research sought to better understand the needs of this special population in order to develop more targeted and customized guides.

This research was developed through the training and support provided by the MLA Research Training Institute (RTI) and made possible in part by the Institute of Museum and Library Services (IMLS) Laura Bush 21st Century Librarian Program (RE-246568-OLS-20).
