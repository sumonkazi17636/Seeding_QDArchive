"Seed systems in West Africa"

Please cite any data used from this data project as follows: 

Jones, Kristal. 2019. "Seed systems in West Africa". Qualitative Data Repository. https://doi.org/10.5064/F6URYY1I. QDR Main Collection.

This project includes audio recordings, digital transcripts, photographs, and tabular sheets of quantitative data, all of which were gathered as part of a mixed methods research project that looked at how local seed systems in Francophone West Africa were impacted by the introduction of 'modern' seed varieties and formal seed markets. The data were gathered over a two-year (for all qualitative data) or three-year (for quantitative data) period, from 2011 to 2013. Data collection was done by a primary researcher, with local research assistants and translators.
