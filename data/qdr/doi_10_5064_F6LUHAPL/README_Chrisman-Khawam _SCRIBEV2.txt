**Title**
Data for: How Pre-Medical Scribing Shapes Medical Student Experience and Professional Identity

**Suggested Citation**
Please cite any data used from this data project as follows: 

Chrisman-Khawam, Leanne. 2025. "Data for: How Pre-Medical Scribing Shapes Medical Student Experience and Professional Identity". Qualitative Data Repository. https://doi.org/10.5064/F6LUHAPL. QDR Main Collection.V2. 

**Description**
Survey exploring medical student attitude and impressions of the impact of medical scribing on their admissions, their initial learning and professional development.  The surveys include a generated survey with both quantitative and qualitative responses on the impact of medical scribing on student admission and impact on early learning and clinical learning compared to peers and a known and validated survey, the professional identity survey.
