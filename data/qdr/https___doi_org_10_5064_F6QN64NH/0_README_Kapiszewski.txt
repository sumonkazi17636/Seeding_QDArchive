The Argentine Supreme Court in Press, 1995-1999.




Please cite any data used from this collection as follows: 



Kapiszewski, Diana. (2015) “The Argentine Supreme Court in Press, 1995-1999.” Data Collection, QDR:10046. Syracuse, NY: Qualitative Data Repository [distributor]. http://doi.org/10.5064/F6QN64NH

The data are related to a broader project which analyzes the strikingly different patterns of inter-branch interaction over economic governance that emerged in Argentina and Brazil during the two decades following regime transition.  The centerpiece of the shared data is the Excel spreadsheet listing 2,037 newspaper articles concerning the Argentine Supreme Court that appeared in the Argentine daily La Nación between 1995 and 1999.
