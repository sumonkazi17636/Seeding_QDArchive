**Title**

Online Appendices for: "Insurgent Recruitment Practices and Combat Effectiveness in Civil War: The Black September Conflict in Jordan"

**Suggested Citation**
Please cite any materials used from this data project as follows: 

Plapinger, Samuel. 2022. "Online Appendices for: "Insurgent Recruitment Practices and Combat Effectiveness in Civil War: The Black September Conflict in Jordan"". Qualitative Data Repository. https://doi.org/10.5064/F617CWDH. Security Studies. 

**Description**

These appendices provide supplementary information and background for the corresponding article published in Security Studies. The appendices include: (a) an extended discussion of the fieldwork that informed the article; (b) methodology and coding information for variables used in the article; (c) documentation for the killed-in-action (KIA) figures used in the article; (d) a list of the archival sources consulted for the article; and (e) a list of additional references on the Jordanian Civil War.
