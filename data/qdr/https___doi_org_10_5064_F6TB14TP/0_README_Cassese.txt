Monstrosity and Dehumanization in the 2016 U.S. Presidential Contest




Please cite any data used from this collection as follows: 



Cassese, Erin C. (2017) Data for: "Monstrosity and Dehumanization in the 2016 U.S. Presidential Contest." Data Collection, QDR:10079. Syracuse, NY: Qualitative Data Repository [distributor]. https://doi.org/10.5064/F6TB14TP? 

This research project argues that monstrosity was used as a metaphor to convey the dangerous potential of the 2016 Presidential race candidates and to challenge their moral standing. The text and images included in this collection were gathered on an ad hoc basis as examples of monstrosity and dehumanization in the discourse surrounding the 2016 Presidential race.
